[1] We propose an effective content enriching method for microblog, to enhance classification accuracy.. [2] News corpus is exploited as external knowledge.. [3] As for techniques, our method uses LDA as its topic analysis model and formulates topic inference for new data as convex optimization problems.. [4] Compared with traditional representation, enriched microblog shows great improvement in classification tasks.. [5] As we do not control the quality of added words, our future work starts from building a filter to select better additional information.. [6] And to make the most of external knowledge, better ways to build topic space should be considered..
