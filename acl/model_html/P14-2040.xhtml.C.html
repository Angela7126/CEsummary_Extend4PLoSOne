[0] This paper presents a method for detecting words related to a topic (we call them topic words) over time in the stream of documents. [1] Topic words are widely distributed in the stream of documents, and sometimes they frequently appear in the documents, and sometimes not. [2] We propose a method to reinforce topic words with low frequencies by collecting documents from the corpus, and applied Latent Dirichlet Allocation [ 4 ] to these documents. [3] For the results of LDA, we identified topic words by using Moving Average Convergence Divergence. [4] In order to evaluate the method, we applied the results of topic detection to extractive multi-document summarization. [5] The results showed that the method was effective for sentence selection in summarization. [6] The research described in this paper explores a method for detecting topic words over time in series of documents. [7] The results of extrinsic evaluation showed that integration of LDA and MACD is effective for topic detection.
