[1] In this paper, we report our preliminary efforts in building an English-Turkish parallel treebank corpus for statistical machine translation.. [2] In the corpus, we manually generated parallel trees for about 5,000 sentences from Penn Treebank.. [3] English sentences in our set have a maximum of 15 tokens, including punctuation.. [4] We constrained the translated trees to the reordering of the children and the replacement of the leaf nodes with appropriate glosses.. [5] We also report the tools that we built and used in our tree translation task..
