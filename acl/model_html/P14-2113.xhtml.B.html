[1] We present a sentiment analysis-based approach to online dispute detection.. [2] We create a large-scale dispute corpus from Wikipedia Talk pages to study the problem.. [3] A sentiment prediction model based on isotonic CRFs is proposed to output sentiment labels at the sentence-level.. [4] Experiments on our dispute corpus also demonstrate that classifiers trained with sentiment tagging features outperform others that do not..
