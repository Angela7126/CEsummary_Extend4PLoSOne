[1] Automatic extraction of new words is an indispensable precursor to many NLP tasks such as Chinese word segmentation, named entity extraction, and sentiment analysis.. [2] This paper aims at extracting new sentiment words from large-scale user-generated content.. [3] We propose a fully unsupervised, purely data-driven framework for this purpose.. [4] We design statistical measures respectively to quantify the utility of a lexical pattern and to measure the possibility of a word being a new word.. [5] The method is almost free of linguistic resources (except POS tags), and requires no elaborated linguistic rules.. [6] We also demonstrate how new sentiment word will benefit sentiment analysis..
