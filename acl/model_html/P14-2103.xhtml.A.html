[1] This paper introduces an unsupervised graph-based method that selects textual labels for automatically generated topics.. [2] Our approach uses the topic keywords to query a search engine and generate a graph from the words contained in the results.. [3] PageRank is then used to weigh the words in the graph and score the candidate labels.. [4] The state-of-the-art method for this task is supervised [ 14 ].. [5] Evaluation on a standard data set shows that the performance of our approach is consistently superior to previously reported methods..
