[0] This paper demonstrates the importance of relation equivalence for entity translation pair discovery. [1] Existing approach of understanding relation equivalence has focused on using explicit features of co-occurring entities. [2] In this paper, we explore latent features of temporality for understanding relation equivalence, and empirically show that the explicit and latent features complement each other. [3] Our proposed hybrid approach of using both explicit and latent features improves relation translation by 0.16 F1-score, and in turn improves entity translation by 0.02. [4] This paper studied temporality features for relation equivalence. [5] With the proposed features, we devised a hybrid approach combining corpus latent and explicit features with complementary strength. [6] We empirically showed the effectiveness of our hybrid approach on relation translation, and it, in turn, improved entity translation.
