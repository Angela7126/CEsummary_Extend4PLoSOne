[1] Chinese is an ancient hieroglyphic.. [2] It is inattentive to structure.. [3] Therefore, segmenting and parsing Chinese are more difficult and less accurate.. [4] In this paper, we propose an Omni-word feature and a soft constraint method for Chinese relation extraction.. [5] The Omni-word feature uses every potential word in a sentence as lexicon feature, reducing errors caused by word segmentation.. [6] In order to utilize the structure information of a relation instance, we discuss how soft constraint can be used to capture the local dependency.. [7] Both Omni-word feature and soft constraint make a better use of sentence information and minimize the influences caused by Chinese word segmentation and parsing.. [8] We test these methods on the ACE 2005 RDC Chinese corpus.. [9] The results show a significant improvement in Chinese relation extraction, outperforming other methods in F-score by 10% in 6 relation types and 15% in 18 relation subtypes..
