[1] In this paper we apply recursive neural networks to political ideology detection, a problem where previous work relies heavily on bag-of-words models and hand-designed lexica.. [2] We show that our approach detects bias more accurately than existing methods on two different datasets.. [3] In addition, we describe an approach to crowdsourcing ideological bias annotations.. [4] We use this approach to create a new dataset from the ibc , which is labeled at both the sentence and phrase level..
