[1] In this work, we proposed to treat punctuations as properties of context words for dependency parsing.. [2] Experiments with an arc-standard parser showed that our method effectively improves parsing performance and we achieved the best accuracy for single-model transition-based parser.. [3] Regarding punctuation processing for dependency parsing, Li et al.. [4] 2010 ) proposed to utilize punctuations to segment sentences into small fragments and then parse the fragments separately.. [5] A similar approach is proposed by Spitkovsky et al.. [6] 2011 ) which also designed a set of constraints on the fragments to improve unsupervised dependency parsing..
