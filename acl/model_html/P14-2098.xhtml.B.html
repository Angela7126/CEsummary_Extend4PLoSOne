[1] A revision often contains multiple corrections that address different writing mistakes.. [2] We explore building computer programs to accurately detect individual corrections in one single revision.. [3] One major challenge lies in determining whether consecutive basic-edits address the same mistake.. [4] We propose a classifier specialized in this task.. [5] Our experiments suggest that.. [6] 1) the proposed classifier reduces correction mis-detections in previous systems by 1/3, leading to significant overall system performance.. [7] 2) our method is generalizable over different data collections..
