[1] We have presented a new learning objective for neural language models that incorporates prior knowledge contained in resources to improve learned word embeddings.. [2] We demonstrated that the Relation Constrained Model can lead to better semantic embeddings by incorporating resources like PPDB, leading to better language modeling, semantic similarity metrics, and predicting human semantic judgements.. [3] Our implementation is based on the word2vec package and we made it available for general use 2 2 https://github.com/Gorov/JointRCM.. [4] We believe that our techniques have implications beyond those considered in this work.. [5] We plan to explore the embeddings suitability for other semantics tasks, including the use of resources with both typed and scored relations.. [6] Additionally, we see opportunities for jointly learning embeddings across many tasks with many resources, and plan to extend our model accordingly..
