[1] This paper demonstrates the importance of relation equivalence for entity translation pair discovery.. [2] Existing approach of understanding relation equivalence has focused on using explicit features of co-occurring entities.. [3] In this paper, we explore latent features of temporality for understanding relation equivalence, and empirically show that the explicit and latent features complement each other.. [4] Our proposed hybrid approach of using both explicit and latent features improves relation translation by 0.16 F1-score, and in turn improves entity translation by 0.02..
