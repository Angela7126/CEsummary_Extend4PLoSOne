[0] Relation extraction suffers from a performance loss when a model is applied to out-of-domain data. [1] This has fostered the development of domain adaptation techniques for relation extraction. [2] This paper evaluates word embeddings and clustering on adapting feature-based relation extraction systems. [3] We systematically explore various ways to apply word embeddings and show the best adaptation improvement by combining word cluster and word embedding information. [4] Finally, we demonstrate the effectiveness of regularization for the adaptability of relation extractors.
