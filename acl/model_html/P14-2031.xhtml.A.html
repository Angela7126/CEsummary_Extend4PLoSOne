[1] In this study, we analyze links between edits in Wikipedia articles and turns from their discussion page.. [2] Our motivation is to better understand implicit details about the writing process and knowledge flow in collaboratively created resources.. [3] Based on properties of the involved edit and turn, we have defined constraints for corresponding edit-turn-pairs.. [4] We manually annotated a corpus of 636 corresponding and non-corresponding edit-turn-pairs.. [5] Furthermore, we show how our data can be used to automatically identify corresponding edit-turn-pairs.. [6] With the help of supervised machine learning, we achieve an accuracy of .87 for this task..
