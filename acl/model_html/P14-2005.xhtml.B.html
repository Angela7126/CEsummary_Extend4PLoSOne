[1] The original BLANC-gold [ 7 ] requires that system mentions be identical to gold mentions, which limits the metric s utility since detected system mentions often have missing key mentions or spurious mentions.. [2] The proposed BLANC is free from this assumption, and we have shown that it subsumes the original BLANC-gold.. [3] Since BLANC works on imperfect system mentions, we have used it to score the CoNLL 2011 and 2012 coreference systems.. [4] The BLANC scores show strong correlation with existing metrics, especially B-cubed and CEAF-m..
