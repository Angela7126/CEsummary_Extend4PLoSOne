[0] Creating cross-language article links among different online encyclopedias is now an important task in the unification of multilingual knowledge bases. [1] In this paper, we propose a cross-language article linking method using a mixed-language topic model and hypernym translation features based on an SVM model to link English Wikipedia and Chinese Baidu Baike, the most widely used Wiki-like encyclopedia in China. [2] To evaluate our approach, we compile a data set from the top 500 Baidu Baike articles and their corresponding English Wiki articles. [3] The evaluation results show that our approach achieves 80.95% in MRR and 87.46% in recall. [4] Our method does not heavily depend on linguistic characteristics and can be easily extended to generate cross-language article links among different online encyclopedias in other languages. [7] We propose a method based on article hypernym and topic model to link English Wikipedia articles to corresponding Chinese Baidu Baike articles. [8] Our method comprises two stages candidate selection and candidate ranking. [9] We formulate candidate selection as a cross-language information retrieval task based on the title similarity between English and Chinese articles. [10] In candidate ranking, we employ several features of the articles in our SVM model. [11] To evaluate our method, we compile a dataset from English Wikipedia and Baidu Baike, containing the 500 most popular Baidu articles. [12] Evaluation results of our method show an MRR of up to 80.95% and a recall of 87.46%. [13] This shows that our method is effective in generating cross-language links between English Wikipedia and Baidu Baike with high accuracy. [14] Our method does not heavily depend on linguistic characteristics and can be easily extended to generate cross-language article links among different encyclopedias in other languages.
