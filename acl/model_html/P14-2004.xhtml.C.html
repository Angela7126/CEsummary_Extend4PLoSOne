[0] Dialog topic tracking aims at analyzing and maintaining topic transitions in on-going dialogs. [1] This paper proposes a composite kernel approach for dialog topic tracking to utilize various types of domain knowledge obtained from Wikipedia. [2] Two kernels are defined based on history sequences and context trees constructed based on the extracted features. [3] The experimental results show that our composite kernel approach can significantly improve the performances of topic tracking in mixed-initiative human-human dialogs. [4] This paper presented a composite kernel approach for dialog topic tracking. [5] This approach aimed to represent various types of domain knowledge obtained from Wikipedia as two structures history sequences and domain context trees; then incorporate them into the model with kernel methods. [6] Experimental results show that the proposed approaches helped to improve the topic tracking performances in mixed-initiative human-human dialogs with respect to the baseline model.
