[1] Our work aims to help people speak foreign languages they don t know, by providing native phonetic spellings that approximate the sounds of foreign phrases.. [2] We use a cascade of finite-state transducers to accomplish the task.. [3] We improve the model by adding phrases, word boundary constraints, and improved alignment.. [4] In the future, we plan to cover more language pairs and directions.. [5] Each target language raises interesting new challenges that come from its natural constraints on allowed phonemes, syllables, words, and orthography..
