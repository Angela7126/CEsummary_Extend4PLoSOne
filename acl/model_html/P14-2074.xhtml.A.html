[1] Image description is a new natural language generation task, where the aim is to generate a human-like description of an image.. [2] The evaluation of computer-generated text is a notoriously difficult problem, however, the quality of image descriptions has typically been measured using unigram bleu and human judgements.. [3] The focus of this paper is to determine the correlation of automatic measures with human judgements for this task.. [4] We estimate the correlation of unigram and Smoothed bleu , ter , rouge-su4 , and Meteor against human judgements on two data sets..
