[1] This paper presents a method for detecting words related to a topic (we call them topic words) over time in the stream of documents.. [2] Topic words are widely distributed in the stream of documents, and sometimes they frequently appear in the documents, and sometimes not.. [3] We propose a method to reinforce topic words with low frequencies by collecting documents from the corpus, and applied Latent Dirichlet Allocation [ 4 ] to these documents.. [4] For the results of LDA, we identified topic words by using Moving Average Convergence Divergence.. [5] In order to evaluate the method, we applied the results of topic detection to extractive multi-document summarization.. [6] The results showed that the method was effective for sentence selection in summarization..
