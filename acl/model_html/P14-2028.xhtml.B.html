[1] In this paper we introduced the stochastic iterative correction method for spell check corrections.. [2] Our experimental evaluation showed that the proposed method improved the performance of popular spelling correction approach   the noisy channel model   in the correction of difficult spelling errors.. [3] We showed how to eliminate the local minima issue of simulated annealing and proposed a technique to make our algorithm deterministic.. [4] The experiments conducted on the specialized datasets have shown that our method significantly improves the performance of the correction of hard spelling errors (by 6.6% F 1 ) while maintaining good performance on common spelling errors.. [5] In continuation of the work we are considering to expand the method to correct errors in multi-word queries, extend the method to work with discriminative models, and use a query performance prediction method, which tells for a query whether our algorithm needs to be applied..
