[1] We have presented a simple but effective approach to integrating the sentence skeleton information into a phrase-based system.. [2] The experimental results show that the proposed approach achieves very promising BLEU improvements and TER reductions on the NIST evaluation data.. [3] In our future work we plan to investigate methods of integrating both syntactic models (for skeleton translation) and phrasal models (for full translation) in our system.. [4] We also plan to study sophisticated reordering models for skeleton translation, rather than reusing the baseline reordering model which is learned on the full sentences..
