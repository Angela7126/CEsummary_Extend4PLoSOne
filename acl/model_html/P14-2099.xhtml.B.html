[1] We considered the task of aligning sections of a collection of roughly similarly-structured legal documents, based on the issues they address.. [2] We introduced an unsupervised model for this task along with two new (and reusable) evaluations.. [3] Our experiments show the approach to be more effective than clustering and topic models.. [4] The corpus and evaluation data have been made available at http://usableprivacy.org/data.. [5] In future work, policy section alignments will be used in automated analysis to extract useful information for users and privacy scholars..
