[1] Relation extraction suffers from a performance loss when a model is applied to out-of-domain data.. [2] This has fostered the development of domain adaptation techniques for relation extraction.. [3] This paper evaluates word embeddings and clustering on adapting feature-based relation extraction systems.. [4] We systematically explore various ways to apply word embeddings and show the best adaptation improvement by combining word cluster and word embedding information.. [5] Finally, we demonstrate the effectiveness of regularization for the adaptability of relation extractors..
