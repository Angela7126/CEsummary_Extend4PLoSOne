[0] Things can be viewed from different facets, and all entities are related. [1] Multi-dimensional classifications and social networking are two fundamental aspects of these relations. [2] Understanding the semantics of various interactions can help improve interactions. [3] Linking faceted search, social networking and interactive semantics can deepen our understanding in research on the interactions between humans and information, and create new research directions. [4] This special issue tries to establish this link, and we hope that it can help accelerate research and development towards the future Web.
