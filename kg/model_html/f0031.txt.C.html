[0] All astronomy data and literature will soon be online and accessible via the Internet. [1] The community is building the Virtual Observatory, an organization of this worldwide data into a coherent whole that can be accessed by anyone, in any form, from anywhere. [2] The resulting system will dramatically improve our ability to do multi-spectral and temporal studies that integrate data from multiple instruments. [3] The Virtual Observatory data also provide a wonderful base for teaching astronomy, scientific discovery, and computational science.
