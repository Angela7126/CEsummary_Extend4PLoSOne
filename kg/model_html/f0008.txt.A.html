[0] Classification is the most basic method for organizing resources in the physical space, cyber space, socio space and mental space. [1] To create a unified model that can effectively manage resources in different spaces is a challenge. [2] The Resource Space Model RSM is to manage versatile resources with a multi-dimensional classification space. [3] It supports generalization and specialization on multi-dimensional classifications. [4] This paper introduces the basic concepts of RSM, and proposes the Probabilistic Resource Space Model, P-RSM, to deal with uncertainty in managing various resources in different spaces of the cyber-physical society. [5] P-RSM's normal forms, operations and integrity constraints are developed to support effective management of the resource space. [6] Characteristics of the P-RSM are analyzed through experiments. [7] This model also enables various services to be described, discovered and composed from multiple dimensions and abstraction levels with normal form and integrity guarantees. [8] Some extensions and applications of the P-RSM are introduced.
