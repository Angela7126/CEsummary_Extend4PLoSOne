[0] Big data research has attracted great attention in science, technology, industry and society. [1] It is developing with the evolving scientific paradigm, the fourth industrial revolution, and the transformational innovation of technologies. [2] However, its nature and fundamental challenge have not been recognized, and its own methodology has not been formed. [3] This paper explores and answers the following questions: [4] What is big data? [5] What are the basic methods for representing, managing and analyzing big data? [6] What is the relationship between big data and knowledge? [7] Can we find a mapping from big data into knowledge space? [8] What kind of infrastructure is required to support not only big data management and analysis but also knowledge discovery, sharing and management? [9] What is the relationship between big data and science paradigm? [10] What is the nature and fundamental challenge of big data computing? [11] A multi-dimensional perspective is presented toward a methodology of big data computing.
