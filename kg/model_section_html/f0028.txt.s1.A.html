[0] Scientific instruments and computer simulations are creating vast data with higher qualities that require new scientific methods to analyze and organize the data. [1] Analyzing this data to find the subtle effects missed by previous studies requires algorithms that can simultaneously deal with huge datasets and that can find very subtle effects. [2] We believe that most new science happens when the data is examined in new ways. [3] So our focus here is on data exploration, interactive data analysis, and integration of datasets. [4] Data analysis tools have not kept pace with our ability to capture and store data, and scientists miss the simpler days when a notebook can store all the experiment data. [5] We want to build the smart notebook that will access data presented by science centers that will provide the community with analysis tools and computational resources to explore huge data archives.
