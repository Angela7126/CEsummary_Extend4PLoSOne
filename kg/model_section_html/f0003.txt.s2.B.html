[0] The Cyber-Physical-Physiological-Psychological-Socio-Mental Environment CP3SME is a multi-dimensional complex space that generates and evolves diverse spaces to contain various types of individuals interacting with, reflecting, or influencing each other through various links within space or through spaces. [1] CP3SME has the following distinguished characteristics: [2] (1) Real-time multi-space situation aware. [3] (2) Complex link. [4] Complex link is the composition of semantic link and interactive channels [89]. [5] (3) Complex health evaluation. [6] Health should be detected and evaluated from multiple spaces. [7] (4) Multi-space coordination through times. [8] (5) Pervasive undetermined interaction. [9] (6) Multiple semantic layer reflection and lifetime preserving. [10] (7) Coordinating spaces. [11] CP3SME will coordinate triangle reasoning/navigation, orthogonal locating and zooming. [12] (8) Harmony. [13] CP3SME concerns harmonious development of the cyber space, physical space, physiological space, psychological space, socio space and mental space rather than just the speed and capacity of computing and communication. [14] (9) Reflecting minds. [15] CP3SME reflects minds by establishing and evolving cyber semantic images for various behaviors in multiple spaces [16] (10) Time dimension. [17] Time dimension can be compressed and stretched in the CP3SME. [18] This requires CP3SME to have an elastic time dimension and the models for the evolution of communities and individuals. [19] The following are some revolution aspects of the environment: [20] Scientists will be able to access research objects and thoughts as well as their formation processes on demand through times. [21] They can not only use languages to express ideas but also link the language representations to reasons in multiple spaces, to relevant research, and to applications. [22] Students can learn natural and socio laws not only from linguistic and mathematical description in textbooks but also from the linked physical, physiological, psychological and socio phenomena through times. [23] Learning materials and processes can be self-organized and adapted according to students' real-time interest and psychological statuses. [24] Function, structure, designer, owner, developer, and even ecological, physiological, psychological and socio effects will be accessible. [25] All spaces will cooperatively reflect the formation processes of artifacts when they are required, designed, built, sold, used, and recycled. [26] Individuals, family trees, thoughts, and socio events will be sensed and preserved as cyber semantic images that can be accessed through times. [27] Health of individuals can be detected and evaluated on time, and evaluation results can be linked to measures. [28] The CP3SME will help people with different cultural backgrounds collaborate effectively by transforming symbol forms, linking symbols to different spaces, and establishing peers' semantic images in their mental spaces. [29] The closed loop from production to consumption can be established to minimize the waste of material and energy with awareness of situations in multiple spaces. [30] The CP3SME will help people know pollution sources and take appropriate control measures according to dynamic real-time micro-environment and recommendations. [31] (9) Interactive virtual presence. [32] Energy supplies will link to requirements, road maps, patterns of traffic situation, parking spaces, and environment status. [33] Vehicles can be guided to appropriate destination according to intentions and minimization of energy consumption. [34] The Cyber-Physical-Socio Ecology concerns how to keep a reasonable expansion of the cyber space and the socio space so that they can harmoniously evolve with the physical space otherwise it may form negative influence on the whole environment [64]. [35] The revolution of CP3SME will also bring new security issues.
