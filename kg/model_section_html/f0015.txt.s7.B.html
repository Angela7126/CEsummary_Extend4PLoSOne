[0] It is easiest to understand the ascription of thoughts to machines in circumstances when we also understand the machine in physical terms. [1] However, we must be careful not to ascribe properties to a machine that the particular machine doesn't have. [2] Computers will end up with the psychology that is convenient to their designers. [3] If designers and programmers think about the apparent mental qualities that their programs will have, they'll create programs that are easier and pleasanter -- more humane -- to deal with.
