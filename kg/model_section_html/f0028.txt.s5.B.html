[0] Much science data is in the form of numeric arrays generated by instruments and simulations. [1] Simple and convenient data models have evolved to represent arrays and relationships among them. [2] There are many parallel and competing efforts to build these tool suites. [3] Data interchange outside each group is problematic. [4] Definitions of common terminology (units and measurements) are emerging within each discipline. [5] These efforts will likely start to converge over the next decade -- probably as part of the converged format standard. [6] This will greatly facilitate tool-building and tools. [7] computer-usable ontologies will help build the Semantic Web
