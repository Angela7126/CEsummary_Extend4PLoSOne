[0] Cross-match spatially clusters and organizes several astronomical point-source measurements from one or more surveys. [1] In this process, we need to deal with missing observations caused by the change of observation conditions and the objects themselves. [2] A solution consists three steps: [3] 1) Classify misses into three types. [4] 2) Transitive closure clusters friends-of-friends into object bundles. [5] 3) Record the bundle summary statistics in a Bundle table.
