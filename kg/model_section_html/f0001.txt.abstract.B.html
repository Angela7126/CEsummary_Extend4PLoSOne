[0] Summarization plays an important role in understanding and representation. [1] Text summarization has been studied for over half century, but it is still hard to automatically generate a satisfied summary. [2] This paper summarizes previous text summarization approaches in a multi-dimensional classification space, introduces a multi-dimensional methodology for research and development, unveils the basic characteristics and principles of language use and understanding, investigates some fundamental mechanisms of summarization, studies the dimensions and forms of representations, and proposes a multi-dimensional evaluation mechanisms. [3] Investigation extends to the incorporation of pictures into summary and to the summarization of videos, graphs and pictures, and then reaches a general summarization framework. [4] (1) a representation suitable for summarization should have a core, indicated by its intention and extension; [5] (2) summarization is an open process of various interactions, involved in various explicit and implicit citations; [6] and, (3) the form of summary is diverse and summarization carries out through multiple dimensions.
