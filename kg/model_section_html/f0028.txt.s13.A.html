[0] The following are some hints of success: [1] 1) Focus on the ability of databases to simplify and speedup the production of raw simulation data. [2] The speedup can be achieved by better indexing and parallelism. [3] 2) Try to add user defined functions and stored procedures to databases, and pay attention to manage petabyte stores of data. [4] 3) Allow queries to define more sophisticated mesh partitions and allow concurrent indexed access to the simulation data for visualization and computational steering. [5] 4) Add visualization tools to watch and steer the computation by reading and writing the database. [6] 5) Focus on providing declarative data analysis interfaces, with which scientists spend most of their time.
