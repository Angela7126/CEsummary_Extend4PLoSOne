[0] There are different viewpoints on text understanding. [1] Any method is limited in its inventor's knowledge and understandings of problems. [2] Integrating different methods is a way to break the limitations. [3] Various methods can be organized in a multi-dimensional methodological space as shown in Figure 2. [4] A complex methodology is regulated by a subspace or a point, which has a projection at every dimension of the methodology space. [5] This paper attempts to explore the problem of summarization from multiple dimensions (especially from empiricism, rationalism, evolutionism, and social/individual constructionism) so as to form a general summarization methodology.
