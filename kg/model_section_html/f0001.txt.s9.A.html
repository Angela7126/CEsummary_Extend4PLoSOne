[0] Summarization is a kind of operation that inputs one or more representations in a representation space and then outputs a new representation. [1] The way human processing representations indicates human mind uniformly processes various things at certain cognitive level. [2] This indicates the possibility of creating a unified method for processing different objects such as texts, pictures, videos and graphs. [3] A unified representation should reflect the most fundamental characteristics of concerned representations. [4] Establishing a unified representation enables a summarization system to uniformly process various representations. [5] A unified method for summarizing different forms can be developed by transforming texts, videos, pictures and graphs into semantic link networks. [6] The form of summary can be a small semantic link network of texts, videos, audios, pictures and graphs. [7] Transformation between representations is a way to realize unification, and summarization is a special transformation that operates dimension reduction for easier understanding. [8] That is, the dimension of a summary should be same as the dimension of its reader's cognitive architecture. [9] The cognitive hierarchy of different communities corresponds to different sub-graphs of the hierarchy of universal concepts, i.e, cognitive levels. [10] A summary is suitable if its cognitive level is the same as the reader's cognitive level. [11] (1) If the original representation's cognitive level is the same as the reader's, the summary should use the core representations in the original representation. [12] (2) If the representation's is higher, the summary should use more specific concepts in the commonsense category hierarchy. [13] (3) If the representation's is lower, the summary should use more general concepts [14] Ontology helps explain representation and establish the links between representations. [15] Representations can be generalized, united, classified and semantically linked to form a lattice of representations at a cognitive level of ontology. [16] The general summarization inputs a representation and a cognitive level at certain ontology and then outputs a representation lattice and a recommended representation.
