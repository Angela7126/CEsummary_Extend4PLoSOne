[0] Automatic text summarization can solve the conflict between the expansion of texts in cyberspace and the limitation of readers' time and energy. [1] The basic idea of text summarization is to distill the most important language representation units from a text to produce an abridged version for particular task and user. [2] That is, automatic summarization systems need to consider three issues, which are selecting important language units, ordering the extracted units, and composing units into fluent texts. [3] Previous automatic summarization methods can be classified by input, output, techniques, and evaluation criteria. [4] A multi-dimensional classification space which includes above classification dimensions can be used to summary all automatic summarization methods. [5] Many of previous methods have the following three characteristics that may not suitable. [6] (1) From text to text. [7] They cannot use words that are not important in current text, or beyond current text. [8] (2) Automation. [9] Their summarization process excludes humans and cannot take enough human experiences and knowledge into consideration. [10] (3) Closed system. [11] Their process of automatic summarization does not interact with other processes in cyberspace or social space. [12] Therefore, we need to consider many questions to form the methodology for developing summarization research. [13] The first is how to enable summarizers to summary a representation from different dimensions. [13] It is necessary because humans lives in a multi-dimensional space, while a good summarizer enables people to quickly know the general and important information.
