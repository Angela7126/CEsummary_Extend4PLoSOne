[0] In this paper, we summarizes previous approaches and explores the fundamental theory and method for summarization from multiple dimensions. [1] There are three core viewpoints: [2] (1) Summarization is carried out with various interactions involved in human, machine, and various representations including text, picture, and video. [3] (2) Writers and readers represent mainly through explicit or implicit citations. [4] (3) Studying the summarization of pictures, videos and graphs reaches a general summarization method.
