[0] Much science data is in the form of numeric arrays generated by instruments and simulations. [1] Simple and convenient data models have evolved to represent arrays and relationships among them. [2] There are many parallel and competing efforts to build these tool suites in each discipline. [3] However, this is problematic for data interchanging outside each group. [4] Therefore, definitions of common terminology (units and measurements) are emerging within each discipline. [5] These efforts will likely start to converge to form a format standard over the next decade. [6] This will greatly facilitate tool-building and tools. [7] Besides, formalizing computer-usable ontologies will also help build the Semantic Web
