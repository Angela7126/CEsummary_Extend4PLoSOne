[0] The summary with pictures is more attractive than the text-only summary. [1] Summaries incorporating pictures can enhance reader's short-term and long-term memory. [2] Further, incorporating pictures into summary can also provide a different bridge between language units, which will enhance the readability of a summary. [3] According to semantic locality principle [Zhuge, 2010], pictures should be selected according to the core sentences and arranged near the core sentences. [4] The following are some problems and strategies for incorporating pictures into summary. [5] (1) How to select appropriate pictures? [6] The semantic link networks of texts, pictures, tags and users indicate a kind of social semantics of picture usage and thus provide the ground for selection. [7] (2) How to organize pictures and texts? [8] a) Use pictures to replace the corresponding representations, to summarize the rest representation, and to organize the summary according to the original structure. [9] b) Select and use pictures to replace the texts in summary. [10] c) Select and insert pictures into summary at appropriate places. [11] d) Classifying pictures from multiple dimensions to facilitate the selection of appropriate pictures. [12] e) Construct a semantic link network of pictures, tags and language representations in relevant texts as the summary. [13] (3) How to identify events in pictures and link them to appropriate texts? [14] The strategy is to make use of sensors and create semantic links between pictures and texts by detecting common projections at physical and social dimensions. [15] According to the efficiency principle and regional principle, the radius of the network should be small. [16] The radius can be defined as the maximum length of link chain from the center (determined by core nodes) to any node. [17] Incorporating pictures into a summary also enables a summarizer to summarize events. [18] And events can be organized by a complex space including dimensions of time, location, people, category and representation. [19] Moreover, pictures can be extended to the snapshots of videos.
