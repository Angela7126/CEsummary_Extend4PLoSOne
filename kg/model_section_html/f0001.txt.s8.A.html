[0] Automatic video summarization can help humans quickly know the key content in big video volumes. [1] It is important in video management, retrieval and browsing. [2] There are two types of video summarization: [3] (1) focusing on still picture to extract a collection of images; [4] and (2) focusing on moving pictures and corresponding audio to extract a short clip. [5] Summarizing a video requires machines to identify the essential characteristics of the video. [6] These characteristics include the simplification of motion, the patterns of events, conversations and behaviors, the low-level features such as color and boundary, and the characteristics of important sections in some particular applications. [7] Current online movies contain subtitles, which provide a new condition for making summarization of videos through natural language processing. [8] Graphs are the generalization of various real networks in cyberspace (e.g., data structures), physical space (e.g., supply chains) and social space (e.g., social networks). [9] Summarizing data structures can provide more general data services. [10] Summarizing a large-scale graph of data is important to graph data management as it can render the patterns hidden in data. [11] For pictures summarization, the main purpose is to generate a small set of pictures from a large set of pictures according to interest. [12] Automatic summarization of pictures can be extended to the following issues: [13] (1) Generating a piece of text to represent a set of pictures. [14] (2) Selecting one picture to represent a set of related pictures. [15] (3) Generating a small network of pictures from a large set of related pictures. [16] (4) Generating a small network of texts according to a large set of pictures. [17] The summarization of pictures can be extended to a four dimensional space -- language, feature, time and location, since model cameras provide time and location information. [18] If we regard a text as a graph of words or sentences, text summarization can be regarded as summarizing a semantic link network. [19] Constructing a semantic structure on videos or pictures by introducing semantic links will also facilitate videos or pictures summarization. [20] Thus the problem of summarizing texts, pictures, and videos can be generalized as one. [20] The form of summary can be a semantic link network of texts, pictures, audios, and videos.
