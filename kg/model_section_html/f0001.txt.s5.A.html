[0] Structuring summary in an appropriate form is important as summary is mainly for human to read and understand. [1] There are some dimensions for organizing representations in a summary, such as time order, authors' organizing patterns and closeness to core representations [2] For organizing a set of closely relevant representations, we should consider the formation process of the set. [3] For multi-document summarization, citations among documents should also be considered. [4] Further, we can consider organize representations with a multi-dimensional classification space. [5] For example, a three dimensional space consisting of time, author and topic dimensions is practical. [6] Generalization and specialization threads through a topic tree. [7] A multi-dimensional space can be represented in different forms, for example, like wind-rose plot. [8] Each reader has his own personal space. [9] A summarization can be satisfied only when it matches the personal space of the reader. [10] Therefore summarization system should summary on readers' demand in an interaction environment. [11] Besides, the form of displaying significantly influences the understandability of summary. [12] Interface innovation enables users to easily understand a summary. [13] We proposed some possible forms of interface. [14] Such as simple structures (list, tree, grid), hypertext, semantic link network, multimedia, pictures or videos emerging and new devices (3D monitor, 3D printer). [15] Whatever you choose, the form of interface should be able to convey the core meaning of representations.
