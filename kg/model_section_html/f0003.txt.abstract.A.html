[0] Machines have two fundamental limitation: [1] (1) They know little link and rules in the physical, physiological, psychological, socio and mental space, so they are not able to discover laws and solve problems in these spaces; [2] (2) They cannot go beyond the cyber space, to learn linking rules, to know the effect of linking, and to explain computing results according to physical, physiological, psychological and socio laws. [3] Linking various spaces will create the Cyber-Physical-Physiological-Psychological-SocioMental Environment CP3SME. [4] From multi-disciplinary perspective, this paper reviews previous ideas on various links, introduces the concept of cyberphysical society, proposes the ideal of the CP3SME including its definition, characteristics, and multi-disciplinary revolution, and explores the methodology of linking through spaces for cyber-physical-socio intelligence.
